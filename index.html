The Basics

Clear subject line. Recipient should know the topic immediately.
One email = one topic. Don't bury multiple requests.
Front-load the ask. State your purpose in first 2 sentences.
Keep it short. 5 sentences or less when possible.
Make action items obvious. Bold or bullet them.

Templates

Request:

Subject: Request: [Specific thing] by [Date] Hi [Name], I'm working on [project] and need [specific thing] to move forward. Could you [specific action] by [date]? Let me know if you have questions. Thanks, [Your name]

Follow-up:

Subject: Following up: [Original topic] Hi [Name], I wanted to follow up on my email from [date] about [topic]. [One sentence reminder of what you need] Would [specific date/time] work to discuss? Thanks, [Your name]

Thank you:

Subject: Thank you for [specific thing] Hi [Name], Thank you for [specific thing they did]. [One sentence about impact or appreciation] [Optional: next steps] Best, [Your name]
